He soon gets involved with the girl named Chloe Decker, a human blessed by God, because he finds out that his powers isn't effective agains't her which in turn, makes him assists her in solving tricky criminal cases, to be with her more and know the mystery behind the immunity against his power.

On the left side, we have trailer of The Witcher, a Polish-American fantasy action drama streaming television series produced by Lauren Schmidt Hissrich. It is based on the book series of the same name by Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski. The witcher named Geralt, a mutated monster hunter, struggles to find his place because of the discrimination in a world in which people often prove more wicked than beasts. Witchers are once-normal humans who got mutated by a combination of medieval science and magic, which made them superhuman, which in turns makes them more suitable to be a monster hunter in a world where monster roams and commits disturbance anywhere.
